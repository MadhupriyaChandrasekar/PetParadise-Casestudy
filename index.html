Problem
Purchasing the item online is something simple, yet purchasing the pet animal online is a troublesome thing.
Goal:
The goal is to create a seamless and compassionate platform that facilitates the process of connecting prospective pet owners with shelters, breeders, and individuals offering pets for adoption or sale. The app aims to prioritize user experience by fostering trust, ensuring transparency in transactions, promoting responsible pet ownership practices, and ultimately enhancing the overall well-being and happiness of both pets and their new owners.
Solution
Pet selling and adoption platform that would function seamlessly across different devices.

Usability study
Summary
In conducting a usability study for a pet adoption and selling app,the focus was on evaluating the user experience (UX) to ensure a seamless and intuitive platform for both adopting and selling pets.The findings revealed that the app's user interface was visually appealing and the registration process straightforward, reducing user friction. The study highlighted the significance of post-adoption support and community engagement, suggesting that incorporating resources for pet owners and fostering a sense of community could enhance the overall user experience of the app.
